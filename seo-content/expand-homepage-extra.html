RoCrack Commitment to User Privacy & Data Protection

In an era where data privacy is paramount, RoCrack maintains a strict privacy-first approach to user data. Our platform operates on the principle of data minimization — we collect only the absolute minimum information needed to provide our services, and we never sell, share, or misuse user data for any purpose beyond service delivery.

When you use RoCrack, here is what we do and do not collect:

  • We collect your Roblox Username — This is necessary to generate Delta Executor keys. It is publicly available information.
  • We DO NOT collect passwords — Your Roblox password is never requested, transmitted, or stored. Period.
  • We DO NOT collect personal identity data — No real names, addresses, phone numbers, or government IDs.
  • We DO NOT track your in-game activity — What scripts you use and how you use them is entirely your business.
  • We use encrypted connections — All data transmitted between your device and RoCrack servers uses TLS encryption.
  • We do not install trackers — Delta Executor does not contain analytics, telemetry, or tracking software beyond what is necessary for the auto-update system.

This commitment to privacy is fundamental to the trust relationship between RoCrack and our users. When 15 million people trust you with their gaming experience, protecting their privacy and security is not optional — it is a sacred responsibility that drives every technical and policy decision we make.
